Open Michigan

Problem Statement


What are the best practices for making OER content accessible to all and what measures can Open Michigan take to comply to WCAG 2.0 guidelines?

Solution


An efficient workflow which takes content of varied forms and determines the steps for making each type of content accessible.

Project Details


Duration:
 September 2016 - January 2017

Role:
 UX Researcher

Key Questions


The goal of the project was to understand best practices for making our OER content on the Open Michigan website accessible, and develop a workflow to ensure that all future content is made accessible when it is posted to the general public.

The research was aimed at finding answers to the following key questions.

  • How do other initiatives/programs/institutions/organizations ensure content is accessible?
  • Does a set of best practices in accessibility exist for the content types we work with on Open Michigan?
  • What are the most important pieces to check for when ensuring an object is accessible?
  • What is a suggested workflow for ensuring that accessibility is appropriately handled during the content intake process?
  • What is a suggested workflow for ensuring that existing content is made as accessible as possible?

Outcome


A report which outlined best practices for each content type and the next steps for ensuring accessible content as per WCAG 2.0 guidelines was prepared. A workflow was created to modify common legacy content types to make them accessible.
